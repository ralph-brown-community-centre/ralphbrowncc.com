Programs

Free Play

An after school Free Drop-In Program for children between of 6-12 years old.

🏀 Free Play Program

We provide actvities such as arts and craft, indoor/outdoor games, fieldtrips and free snack for children. It is run by City Of Winnipeg.
  • 🕒 Hours of Operation
  • Mon 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Tue 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Wed 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Thu 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Fri 3:00pm-6:00pm
GAP

Free program for children to express themselves through art, music and dance.

🎨 GraffitiArt Programming Inc.

We partner with GAP to allow our children to explore their creative side. Encourage young artist to be involved in our community development.
  • 🕔 Hours of Operation
  • Tue 5:00pm-6:00pm
  • Thu 5:00pm-6:00pm
Speak Cree

Free language opportunity for youth and adults. Teach or Learn how to speak Cree.

📚 Speak Cree

Organized by AYO! (Aboriginal Youth Opportunities) Every Tuesday, we have convertional Cree language opportunity for everyone.
  • 🕡 Hours of Operation
  • Tue 6:30pm-8:00pm
Bike Camp

Every summer we have programs and events like Bike Camp and Bike Bazaar for our community.

🚲 Bike Camp

With our partner The Wrench (Winnipeg Repair Education and Cycling Hub), We have an opportunity to have programs for bicycle lovers. Program such as building, repairing and maintaining bicycles.

Skating

Our Skating Rink is available every winter season. It is Free! Everyone is welcome to skate!

⛸ Ice Skating

From our local community who generously donate skating shoes, hockey sticks and helmet; children and youth are able to use and rent skating equipment for free.
