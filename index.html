Featured Programs

🏀 Free Play Drop In Program

After school drop-in program is for children between ages of 6-12 years old. We provide actvities such as arts and craft, indoor/outdoor games, fieldtrips and free snack for children. It is run by City Of Winnipeg.

  • 🕒 Hours of Operation
  • Mon 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Tue 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Wed 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Thu 3:00pm-6:00pm
  • Fri 3:00pm-6:00pm

🎨 GraffitiArt Programming Inc. Art and Dance Club

We partner with GAP to allow our youth to explore their creative side in a fun and safe way!

  • 🕔 Hours of Operation
  • Tue 4:30pm-6:00pm
  • Thu 4:00pm-6:00pm

📚 Speak Cree

Free language opportunity for youth and adults. Teach or Learn how to speak Cree. Organized by AYO! (Aboriginal Youth Opportunities) We have convertional Cree language opportunity for everyone.

  • 🕡 Hours of Operation
  • Tue 6:30pm-8:00pm
